About Dungeons and Dragons

Dungeons and Dragons was originally designed by Gary Gygax and Dave Arneson and was first published in 1974. It was first published by Tactical Studies Rules, Inc but has since then been published by Wizards of the Coast. It was first played as the game miniature wargames with variations from the 1971 game Chainmail. It has been recognized as the beginning of modern role-playing games and the industry.



It had early successes, which then led others to try to create similar games. Even though people have tried to make something similar D&D has remained the market leader in the role-playing industry. Even though there have been different versions it has still remained the best-known and best-selling role-playing game in the U.S. Now you are able to buy games based off of shows that have gained a ton of attention such as Rick and Morty and Stranger Things.
